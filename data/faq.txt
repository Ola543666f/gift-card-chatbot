Q: How do I redeem my gift card?
A: To redeem your gift card, go to the 'Redeem' section in the app and enter your 6-character code. The credit will be applied to your account immediately.

Q: What if I lost my gift card code?
A: If you've lost your gift card code, please contact customer support with your purchase receipt. We cannot retrieve codes through the chatbot for security reasons.

Q: How long are gift cards valid?
A: Gift cards are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.

Q: Where can I buy gift cards?
A: Gift cards can be purchased on our website, in the mobile app, or at any of our retail partners.

Q: Can I use multiple gift cards?
A: Yes, you can combine up to 3 gift cards for a single purchase.

Q: Is there a fee for gift cards?
A: No, there are no activation or maintenance fees for gift cards.

Q: Can I get a refund for my gift card?
A: Gift cards are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person.

Q: How do I check my gift card balance?
A: You can check your balance by entering your gift card code in the 'Check Balance' section.
